Methodology. 30 s eyes-closed baseline establishes per-channel, per-band mean μ and SD σ of log-power. Live readings become z-scores. All windows are kept; running statistics dilute transient blinks and movement. Per-channel contact-quality dots (green < 40 µV RMS, amber < 90 µV, red ≥ 90 µV) flag a single bad electrode rather than blanket-discrediting the headset.

Multi-state analyzer. Each window is scored into five states from the meditation literature: Relaxed (temporal α↑, frontal β↓; Cahn & Polich 2006), Focused (frontal-midline θ↑, β↓; Aftanas & Golocheikine 2001), Aroused (β↑, α↓), Drowsy (δ+θ↑ without α enhancement), Neutral. Plus indices: FAA (Coan & Allen 2004), θ/β, α/β, engagement (Pope, Bogart & Bartolome 1995).
